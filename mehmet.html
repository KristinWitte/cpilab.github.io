Mehmet Yörüten

Research Student

mehmet.yoerueten@tuebingen.mpg.de

Mehmet joined our lab in March 2022 as a MSc student from the Neural Information Processing program at the University of Tübingen. He did his undergraduate degree in Psychology along with a minor in Film Studies at Bogazici University. Mehmet works on perceptual hierarchical segmentation together with Shuchen, where he hopes to understand how people hierarchically segment natrual scenes.
